### Headline

### Lead Paragraph

Personal CRMs were built for businesses. SOCOS is built for people who want to actually keep in touch with the humans in their life — and have an AI agent make sure it happens. No more letting friendships fade because you forgot to respond. No more realizing months too late that you haven't texted someone back.

### The Problem

- 65% of Americans feel isolated despite being more connected than ever
- The average person struggles to maintain more than 150 relationships (Dunbar's number)
- Existing CRMs are built for sales teams, not for people who just want to be better friends

### 5 Key Features

| Feature | What It Means For You |
|---------|----------------------|
| 🤖 **AI Agent Automation** | Your personal relationship agent tracks who needs attention, auto-generates reminders, and enriches contact info — without you lifting a finger |
| 🎮 **Gamification (XP & Levels)** | Earn XP for every interaction, level up your social skills from Level 1–100, unlock achievements, and build streaks that keep you accountable |
| 📊 **Contact Health Scores** | See at a glance which relationships are thriving and which are going dormant — before it's too late |
| 🎂 **Celebration Reminders** | Never forget a birthday, anniversary, or important date again. Your AI agent提前提醒你 |
| 🔓 **Open-Source & Self-Hosted** | Your data stays yours. Deploy on your own server, own your data, privacy-first by design |

### Differentiation

Unlike Monica or Twenty, SOCOS combines the **only** AI-first architecture with **real** gamification — so staying in touch actually feels rewarding instead of like another task on your list.
