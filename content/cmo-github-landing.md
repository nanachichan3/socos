## Problem Section

### Headline

### Body
The average person has 594 dormant contacts in their phone. Not because they don't care — because there's no system that makes keeping in touch rewarding.

Traditional CRMs feel like sales tools. Apple Contacts don't remind you of anything. SOCOS is different: it treats your relationships like a skill you're actively improving.

## How It Works (3 steps)

### Step 1: Add your people
> Upload contacts, import from CSV, or connect Google/Apple contacts. SOCOS builds your relationship map automatically.

### Step 2: Let the agents work
> Relationship Agent tracks who's gone dormant. Reminder Agent surfaces birthdays and check-ins. Enrichment Agent fills in missing contact info from public sources. You don't have to remember — you just respond.

### Step 3: Level up your social skills
> Every interaction logged earns XP. Stay consistent and unlock levels. Keep streaks alive. SOCOS gamifies the habit so you actually stick with it.

## Features Grid

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| 🤖 **AI Agents** | Autonomous agents that track relationship health, remind you proactively, and suggest who to reconnect with |
