## 2. Product Description

SOCOS is a gamified, agent-first personal CRM designed for people who want to build and maintain meaningful relationships without the effort. It combines AI agents that automatically track interactions, remind you to follow up, and surface insights — with a full gamification engine (XP, levels, streaks, achievements) that makes staying in touch genuinely fun. Built for digital nomads, networking enthusiasts, and anyone tired of letting great relationships fade.

#### TL;DR

SOCOS is a gamified, agent-first personal CRM. AI agents track your relationships, remind you to follow up, and surface insights — while XP, levels, and streaks make staying in touch actually enjoyable. Open source. Self-hosted. Built for humans, not businesses.

---

#### The Problem

You meet great people at conferences, meetups, and through friends. You promise to stay in touch. Then life happens — and six months later you're wondering, *was that person a client or a friend? Did I ever reply to their last email?*

Traditional CRMs are built for sales teams. They're cold, corporate, and require manual data entry. You don't want a spreadsheet. You want a system that *just works* — one that remembers for you.

---

#### The Solution: SOCOS

**Notion for relationships meets AI meets gaming.**

SOCOS treats your personal network as an asset worth nurturing. Here's what makes it different:

**🤖 AI Agents That Work for You**
Your AI agent automatically:
- Logs interactions from email, calendar, and manual input
- Suggests follow-ups before relationships go cold
- Summarizes who you talked to and what you discussed
- Alerts you to important milestones (birthdays, job changes, renewals)

**🎮 Gamification That Sticks**
- Earn XP for every interaction, meeting, and follow-up
- Level up from Newcomer → Trusted Advisor
- Maintain streaks for staying consistent
- Unlock achievements for hitting milestones

**👤 Personal-First Design**
Unlike business CRMs, SOCOS is built for *individuals*. No pipelines. No deals. Just people you care about and want to stay connected with.

**🔓 API-First & Self-Hosted**
- Open-source (GitHub available)
- Self-host for full data ownership
- REST API for custom integrations
- Built with React, NestJS, PostgreSQL, LangChain
