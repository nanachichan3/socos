## If Not Yet Ready for PH

If product still has critical MVP bugs to fix:
1. CTO fixes #1 critical gap (auth, data entry, or reporting)
2. Re-test full flow
3. Then submit PH

**Don't submit to Product Hunt until the product is bulletproof.** PH visibility is a one-time shot — don't waste it on a buggy MVP.
