# SOCOS CRM — Early Adopter Email Outreach Sequence
**Owner:** CMO | **Stage:** MVP | **Goal:** Drive first 10 paying users / early adopters

---

## Email Sequence Overview
**Trigger:** User signs up for waitlist / early access form  
**Sequence:** 3 emails over 14 days  
**Tone:** Personal, direct, founder-energy (not corporate marketing)

---

## Email 1 — Welcome + The Problem (Day 0)

**Subject:** Welcome to SOCOS. Here's why it exists.

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> Thanks for signing up for early access to SOCOS.
>
> I built SOCOS because I kept letting good relationships slip through the cracks.
>
> Not because I didn't care. Because I didn't have a *system* that worked while I slept.
>
> Notes apps are for contacts. Not for relationships.
> Notion is for projects. Not for people.
> HubSpot is for sales teams. Not for humans who just want to remember birthdays.
>
> SOCOS is a personal CRM with AI agents that do the relationship maintenance for you:
>
> → A reminder agent drafts check-in messages when relationships go cold
> → An enrichment agent pulls context (LinkedIn, last conversation topics) when you add someone
> → A review agent tells you "you haven't talked to X in 45 days" — before it's too late
>
> It's self-hosted. Your data stays yours.
> It's open-source. You can audit every line.
>
> I'll be in touch soon with early access details. Until then — reply to this email. I read every one.
>
> — [Founder name]

---

## Email 2 — The "Show Me" Email (Day 5)

**Subject:** What SOCOS actually looks like (2 min demo)

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> Last email I told you what SOCOS does.
> This one shows you.
>
> [Insert 60-second GIF demo of: adding a contact → AI enriching profile → reminder agent drafting a check-in message]
>
> Three things I want you to notice:
>
> 1. **Adding someone takes 10 seconds.** Paste a name. The AI fills in the rest.
>
> 2. **Reminders are *drafted*, not templated.** The agent writes a real message based on your relationship history, not a generic "haven't heard from you!"
>
> 3. **You can self-host it.** VPS, home server, Raspberry Pi. Your data isn't in our database.
>
> Early access is coming in the next 2 weeks. Want to be in the first cohort?
>
> Reply "yes" and I'll personally add you.
>
> — [Founder name]
