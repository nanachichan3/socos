## Product Hunt Submission

### Tagline

### Description (250 chars)
> SOCOS is the open-source personal CRM that treats your relationships like a game worth winning. AI agents handle reminders, contact enrichment, and follow-ups — so you can focus on actually showing up.

- 🤖 AI Agents that proactively manage your relationships

- 📊 Relationship health scoring based on interaction frequency
