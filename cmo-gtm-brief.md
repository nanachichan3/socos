# SOCOS CRM — GTM Brief
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-04-26 | **Owner:** CMO

---

## 1. Product & Vision

SOCOS is a gamified, agent-first personal CRM — self-hosted, privacy-first, open source. AI agents proactively manage your relationships (who to reconnect with, when, why). Gamification (XP, levels, streaks) makes relationship maintenance rewarding, not a chore.

**Core differentiator:** The only personal CRM that combines AI agents + gamification + full API access in one self-hosted package.

**Target users:** Solo professionals managing 50–500 relationships; introverts who want to stay in touch but find it cognitively draining; privacy-first users who distrust big tech CRM.

---

## 2. ICP

### Primary: Solo Professionals & Relationship-Focused Individuals
- Coaches, consultants, freelancers, lawyers, therapists managing 50–500 contacts
- Networkers (sales, BD, recruiters) tracking hundreds of touchpoints
- Introverts who genuinely *want* to keep in touch but need a "memory" to help them
- Privacy-first users who want full data ownership

### Secondary
- Long-distance families (adult kids managing aging parent care)
- Personal brand builders (creators, entrepreneurs)
- Small community builders (pastors, teachers)

### Anti-ICP
- Enterprise sales teams
- People who already use a CRM at work
- Team collaboration use cases

---

## 3. Positioning Statement

> *"Your relationships deserve an AI agent."*

Most people let relationships die because they can't remember to follow up. SOCOS has an AI agent that tracks who you should reconnect with, reminds you when you're falling behind, and gives you XP for showing up. It's Notion for relationships meets a personal AI assistant — self-hosted, privacy-first, open source.

**Key differentiators vs. Monica/Twenty:**
- AI Agents that proactively manage relationships (Monica/Twenty: manual entry only)
- Gamification engine (XP, levels, achievements — neither Monica nor Twenty has this)
- API-first architecture (built for agents to interact with)
- Full self-hosting with no external dependencies
