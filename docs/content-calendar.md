## Audience Research

### Target Segments
1. **Solopreneurs & freelancers** — need to manage client relationships without a full sales team
2. **Small business owners** — juggling customer touchpoints across multiple channels
3. **Productivity enthusiasts** — systematizing their personal and professional networks

### Top 3 Content Themes
1. **Never lose a valuable connection** — the fear of forgetting, the pain of disorganization
2. **AI that remembers so you don't have to** — frictionless relationship maintenance
3. **Build your personal knowledge graph** — treating people as data points with context

### Target Audience Pain Points
- Forgetting follow-ups and losing deals
- Scattered notes across WhatsApp, email, notes apps
- No unified view of relationship history with a person
- "I'll remember this" — and then not
- Manually tracking birthdays, preferences, past interactions
- CRM tools feel corporate and overkill for individuals

### 5 Viral Hooks for Personal CRM / Productivity
1. **"I asked 100 professionals what their #1 networking regret was. 80% said the same thing."**
2. **"Your brain is not a CRM. Stop using it like one."**
3. **"This AI remembers every person you've ever met — so you don't have to."**
4. **"The person who could change your life? You already met them. You just forgot."**
5. **"I used to have 47 unread messages. Now I have a second brain for people."**

- LinkedIn posts with images get 2x more engagement — always pair content with visual
- Reddit posts: follow community rules, no direct "check out my product" before engaging genuinely
- Instagram: use story replies to boost algorithmic reach
